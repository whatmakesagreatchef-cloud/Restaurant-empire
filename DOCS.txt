ğŸ“š CORE SYSTEMS EXPLAINED
==========================================

1. WEEKLY OPERATIONS
   - Each week, your restaurants serve customers
   - You earn revenue, pay costs, and see net profit
   - Review weekly reports to spot problems early
   - Take action on urgent issues in the Ops screen

2. MENU MANAGEMENT
   - Design your menu with items that balance profit & popularity
   - Stars: Your money-makers (high profit, high sales)
   - Plowhorses: Popular but lower margin (volume plays)
   - Puzzles: High margin but slow sellers (needs marketing)
   - Dogs: Low profit AND unpopular (remove or fix these!)
   
3. STAFF & MORALE
   - Burnout (0-100): Work stress. Keep below 60.
   - Morale (0-100): Overall happiness. Aim for 70+.
   - Hire managers (GM, Chef, FOH) to delegate and improve operations
   - Train staff to permanently boost skills (worth the investment!)

4. CUSTOMER SATISFACTION
   - Food Quality (38%): Most important factor
   - Service Speed (18%): Fast service = happy customers
   - Value (16%): Fair prices for what they get
   - Vibe (16%): Atmosphere and ambiance
   - Cleanliness (12%): Basic hygiene matters
   
   Target: 70+ satisfaction. Below 60 = complaints & bad reviews.

5. SUPPLY CHAIN
   - Track inventory to avoid stockouts
   - Lock in supplier contracts when prices are good
   - Higher par levels = less stockouts but more waste
   - Do stocktakes to reduce spoilage temporarily

6. FACILITIES & MAINTENANCE
   - Equipment breaks down if not maintained
   - Breakdowns hurt speed, quality, and can cause closures
   - Spend on maintenance to prevent expensive repairs
   - Renovate when condition drops below 50

7. FINANCIALS
   - Food Cost: Target 25-32% of sales
   - Labor Cost: Target 28-38% of sales
   - Net Profit Margin: Aim for 8-15%
   - Keep 3+ months of cash as safety buffer

8. GROWTH & EXPANSION
   - Save profits or take loans to fund new venues
   - Each new venue adds complexity
   - Choose: Chain (one brand) or Portfolio (multiple brands)
   - Economies of scale kick in with 3+ locations
